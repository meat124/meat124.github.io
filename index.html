Hyunjin

I am a researcher in the robotics team at the Machine Learning and Control System (MLCS) lab. I graduated from Yonsei University with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. Previously, I served as the robotics team leader at YAI (Yonsei University Artificial Intelligence club) and received an award from the Minister of Science and ICT.

My research interests focus on Robot Learning, particularly Imitation Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models like pi0.5 and gr00t n1.6. I am also deeply interested in World Models, Diffusion Policy for controlling the 말단 작동기, and developing cross-embodiment transfer frameworks for humanoid robots such as the Rainbow Robotics RB-Y1.
